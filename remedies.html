Chrysanthemum

Used in Chrysanthemum Dragon Rose and Chrysanthemum Romance tea, Chrysanthemum is traditionally used for its beautifying effects (i.e. hydrating the skin and reducing acne; increasing circulation). It is high in antioxidants, fatty acids, and flavinoids and is used to treat high blood pressure, tension, and headaches. It's also used in Chinese medicine to treat the common cold and heat rashes.

×

Cranberry

Found in Cranberry Jasmine Green Tea, cranberries are shown to promote urinary tract health and blood purification. High in polyphenols, which may reduce the risk of cardiovascular disease. Research has also shown that cranberries help slow tumor progression and have positive effects againt prostate, liver, breast, ovarian, and colon cancers. They are high in Vitamin C, Vitamin E, and fiber.

×

Dragon Fruit

Found in Chrysanthemum Dragon Rose Tea, dragon fruit is high in immune-boosting Vitamin C and Vitamin B, which benefits everything from blood pressure, skin health, and cholesteral levels to thyroid funciton and carbohydrate metabolism. Dragon fruit is thought to beautify the skin and calm the mind, and to have antibacterial and antifungal properties.

Ginger

Found in Long Life Ginger Pu'erh Tea and Happy Orange Ginger Tea, ginger is thought to aid digestion, increase circulation, and relieve nausea. It is used as anti-inflammatory remedy, and may reduce muscle pain and soreness. Ginger is also shown to relieve arthritis.

Green Tea

High in catechins, Green Tea is an important antioxidant. Studies have shown that it improves blood flow, lowers cholesteral and blood pressure, and may block plaques in the brain that cause Alzheimer's disease.

×

Oolong Tea

High in polyphenols, which may reduce cholesterol and boost metabolism (and therefore weight loss), oolong tea is also rich in antioxidants. Oolong tea is also shown to relieve eczema by combating free radicals that cause allergic reactions, and contains calcium and magnesium for bone strength.

×

Pu'erh Tea

The fermentation process of pu'erh makes it rich in probiotics, which may aid digestion. Used in Chinese medicine for cardiovascular protection, and to promote weight loss and reduce cholesterol.
